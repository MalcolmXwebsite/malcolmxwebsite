About Malcolm X:

Malcolm X was born in 1925 and he was an American human rights activist who was very popular (and controversial) during the civil rights movement. Some people consider Malcolm X as a man, who was harsh against the white Amercia, despite them also doing very bad things. Others thought that he was a good muslim fighting against the oppression of black people.
